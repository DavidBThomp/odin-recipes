Beef Burger

Description

A beef burger patty is the heart and soul of a classic burger, a culinary icon that has captured the appetites of people worldwide. Crafted from high-quality ground beef, this patty is a celebration of simplicity and taste. Expertly seasoned with a blend of spices and perhaps a touch of salt and pepper, the patty is meticulously shaped to achieve the perfect balance between thickness and juiciness.

What distinguishes a beef burger patty is its versatility, acting as a blank canvas for culinary creativity. From the classic combination of lettuce, tomato, and cheese to more elaborate toppings like crispy bacon, saut√©ed mushrooms, or creamy avocado, the patty serves as the foundation upon which a symphony of flavors can be composed. Whether nestled between two toasted buns or wrapped in a crisp lettuce leaf for a low-carb twist, the beef burger patty remains an emblem of comfort and indulgence.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Combine seasonings into bowl and mix
  2. Shape Patties into 4oz balls
  3. Lightly oil cast iron on med-high heat
  4. Smash balls into 4inch diameter patties on cast iron
  5. Season and flip, season again once flipped.
