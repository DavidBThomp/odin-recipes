Beef Stick Scramble

Description

Elevate your breakfast experience with the hearty and flavorful combination of beef stick in scrambled eggs. This culinary fusion takes the traditional scrambled eggs to a whole new level, infusing them with the rich and savory essence of beef. Thin slices of succulent beef stick, known for their robust taste and satisfying chew, are finely diced and folded into the beaten eggs before they hit the pan. As the eggs cook, the beef stick infuses its savory character into the dish, creating a symphony of tastes that range from the juicy umami of the beef to the creamy tenderness of the eggs.

The marriage of beef stick and scrambled eggs not only results in a culinary delight but also provides a substantial and protein-packed meal to kick-start your day. The combination offers a balance of essential nutrients, including protein from the beef and eggs, ensuring you feel satisfied and energized throughout the morning. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with a slice of toast or a sprinkle of cheese, this beef stick-infused scramble adds a gourmet touch to your breakfast routine, making it a perfect choice for those who crave a hearty and delicious morning meal.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Beef Stick
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • American Cheese

Steps

  1. Whisk 2 eggs
  2. Chop Beef stick and lightly cook on medium heat
  3. Add whisked eggs
  4. Scramble and season
  5. Chop up american cheese and mix in
